03 — Profile

Dil Shrestha

Actor · Producer · Audiographer

Dil Shrestha is one of Nepal's most versatile screen and sound professionals — an actor, independent producer, and audiographer whose decade-long career spans feature films, television, and broadcast media. Born and raised in Kathmandu, he entered the industry with Yatharth (2015) and has since accumulated over two dozen credits across performance, production, and post-production sound.

As an actor, Shrestha is known for measured, character-driven performances — most notably his supporting role in Maya Bhanekai Yesto Ho (2020), which earned him the Box Office Film Award. Behind the lens, he founded his own production house and has shepherded independent Nepali films from script to festival circuit. His rare dual craft — performing and engineering sound on the same project — sets him apart in South Asian cinema.
