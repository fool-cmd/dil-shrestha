BUILT FROM
THE GROUND UP

Dil Shrestha is not a product of privilege — he is a product of persistence. From his early days as a news writer at Radio Sagarmatha to his current position as Managing Director of Shrestha Productions, every step has been earned through relentless work and an unshakeable belief in Nepali storytelling.

With formal training in broadcast journalism and hands-on experience across every layer of media production — from field reporting to executive producing — Dil brings a rare, 360-degree understanding of the industry. He doesn't just act in front of the camera; he understands every frame that surrounds him.

His leadership in production has resulted in content that resonates far beyond Nepal's borders, earning recognition at South Asian film festivals and attracting international co-production interest. As a journalist, his investigative and documentary work has shed light on stories the mainstream often ignores.
